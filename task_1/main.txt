Title: What is Generative AI?

Generative AI is a type of artificial intelligence that creates new content like text, images, audio, and video, based on patterns learned from vast amounts of data. Unlike traditional AI, which analyzes existing data, generative AI is able to produce novel outputs based on user prompts or requests.
